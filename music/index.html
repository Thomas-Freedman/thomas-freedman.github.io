About Me

My first musical experience was playing classical piano as a kid, but I started producing music in high school when my music teacher showed me Reason. In very high school fashion, I mostly made rap beats for my friends at the time, but I spent a long time honing my production and mixing skills culminating in two albums which I released in 2019 and 2020.


I entered UCLA in 2021 as a Music Industry student and began working with artists more seriously. In 2023, I worked as an assistant engineer at Suspect Studios in San Jose, CA. Then, in 2024, I further developed my mixing in both stereo and Dolby Atmos under Grammy-nominated mix engineer Luke Argilla. I'm grateful to have collaborated on so many exciting projects in these roles.


I'm proficient in Pro Tools, Ableton, Reason, stereo and Atmos mixing, and I play piano, guitar, and bass (hopefully drums next!). Though I now spend most of my time on tech development for musical tasks, I still mix and produce for several clients and always enjoy working with new people when I can! Please feel free to reach out by email or the form on my home page.
